# Hey, I'm Klotzkette

Not a developer, not an engineer -- just someone who discovered vibe coding and can't stop tinkering. Everything here is built with AI assistance, curiosity, and a healthy amount of stumbling around in the dark.

---

## Why Vibe-Coding?

As a non-developer, you can finally **visualize** what you actually want -- build it as a working app, and then show the real engineers exactly what you had in mind. Is it IT-secure? No. Would a professional cringe? Probably. But for communicating an idea, it's perfect.
